What do these mean?
  • Dew Point: moisture in air — ~55°F dry; 60–65° a bit humid; 70°+ muggy.
  • Wet Bulb: temp “felt” if sweat evaporates perfectly — crucial for heat stress.
  • T–Td Spread: temp vs dewpoint gap. <5° fog risk; 10–20° comfy; 20°+ dry.
  • VPD: air’s thirst. <0.5 kPa saturated; 0.5–2 moderate; >2 very drying.
  • Pressure: higher ≈ calmer/clear; lower ≈ active/stormy.
